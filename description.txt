
Spirit mod is a huge Terraria mod, with plenty of content to explore, new enemies to fight, and new weapons to wield.
Current content:
- 775 items
- 154 NPCs
- 8 New bosses
- 3 New Minibosses
- 1 New Event!
- 1 Biome
- 1 Mini Biome
- And many secrets for you to find!
Check out the mod's forum page for more information! We hope you enjoy the mod, and please report any issues you encounter :)
